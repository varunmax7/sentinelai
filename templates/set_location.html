How to Set Your Location

Automatic Detection
Click the "Detect My Location" button above. Your browser will ask for location permission. Allow access for automatic coordinate detection.
Google Maps Method
Open Google Maps → Find your location → Right-click on the exact spot → Select "What's here?" → Copy the coordinates from the information panel.
Mobile Apps
Use GPS apps like "GPS Coordinates" or "My Location" on your smartphone to get precise coordinates of your current position.
